# Photographer Plugin for Claude

Create client proposals, shot lists, album descriptions, and marketing emails for photography businesses.

Built by [The AI Career Lab](https://theaicareerlab.com/professions/photographer) — AI tools, guides, and weekly digests designed specifically for photographers.

## Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/client-proposal` | Generate detailed photography service proposals with packages, pricing, timelines, and deliverables |
| `/shot-list` | Create organized shot lists for sessions or events with locations, lighting notes, and priority shots |
| `/album-description` | Write compelling album and gallery descriptions for client delivery, portfolio sites, or publication submissions |
| `/marketing-email` | Draft marketing emails for mini-session promotions, seasonal campaigns, portfolio highlights, and client re-engagement |

## Skills

This plugin includes skills that activate automatically when you're working on photography-related tasks:

- **Photography Business** — Pricing strategies, proposal writing, contracts, client management, and studio operations
- **Visual Storytelling** — Shot composition guidance, album narrative structure, and brand photography direction

## Usage examples

```
/client-proposal
Wedding photography proposal for Emily and James. Date: June 14, 2026.
Venue: Rosewood Estate, outdoor ceremony + indoor reception. They want
8 hours of coverage, second shooter, engagement session, and a 40-page
album. Budget range $4,000-6,000.
```

```
/shot-list
Shot list for a corporate headshot session. 12 executives at a law firm.
On-location in their conference room and lobby. Need consistent lighting
and backdrop across all subjects. Include group shots of the leadership
team and individual portraits.
```

```
/album-description
Album description for a fall family session at Maple Creek Park. Family
of five — parents with three kids ages 2, 5, and 8. Golden hour session,
candid and posed mix. Highlight the autumn colors and natural interactions.
For delivery gallery and portfolio.
```

```
/marketing-email
Marketing email for spring mini-sessions. 20-minute sessions at the
botanical garden, $275 includes 15 edited digital images. Available dates:
April 12 and April 19. Limited to 8 spots per day. Target: existing
clients from the past 2 years.
```

## Disclaimer

All content generated by this plugin is for drafting purposes only. The photographer is responsible for reviewing and customizing all materials to reflect their brand, pricing, and business practices before client use.
