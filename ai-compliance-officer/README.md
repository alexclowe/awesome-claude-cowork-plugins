# AI Compliance Officer Plugin for Claude

Audit AI systems, generate compliance docs, track regulatory change, and evaluate autonomous agents.

Built by [The AI Career Lab](https://theaicareerlab.com/professions/ai-compliance-officer) — AI tools, guides, and weekly digests designed specifically for AI compliance officers.

## Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/audit-ai-system` | Map an AI system against EU AI Act Annex III high-risk criteria and generate a compliance risk assessment with citations |
| `/track-regulatory-change` | Ingest EU/FINRA/FDA/SEC guidance updates, flag impact on deployed systems, and produce a change-impact report |
| `/generate-documentation` | Draft EU AI Act quality management system docs, risk management framework, and conformity assessment package |
| `/eval-autonomous-agent` | Generate a test harness for hallucination, bias, scope creep, and reward misalignment per FINRA 2026 guidelines |

## Skills

This plugin includes skills that activate automatically when you're working on AI compliance tasks:

- **High-Risk System Classification** — Auto-detect EU AI Act Annex III categories (employment, credit, law enforcement, biometrics, education, critical infrastructure) and US-state equivalents
- **Post-Market Monitoring Automation** — Ingest incident reports, cluster patterns, and generate FDA/EU-style remediation recommendations

## Usage examples

```
/audit-ai-system
We deployed a resume-screening model for our 4,000-person org. Trained on 5
years of internal hiring data, used to rank applicants before recruiter review.
Operates in EU and US. Assess Annex III exposure and required controls before
the August 2, 2026 enforcement deadline.
```

```
/track-regulatory-change
Last quarter we logged the EU Commission's high-risk classification guidance
(July 2025), the FINRA autonomous-agent supervisory framework (Q1 2026), and
the SEC Reg AI proposal. Map each to our credit-decisioning model and our
broker-supervision agent. Flag the items that change our control posture.
```

```
/generate-documentation
Draft the EU AI Act Article 17 quality management system documentation for our
fraud-detection model. Include the risk management framework (Article 9), data
governance section (Article 10), and human oversight design (Article 14). Tone
should match a Tier-1 bank's existing model risk management documentation.
```

```
/eval-autonomous-agent
We are deploying a customer-service agent with refund authority up to $500.
Generate the FINRA-aligned eval harness covering hallucination on policy,
scope creep on refund limits, reward hacking on CSAT, and bias on protected
classes. Include red-team prompts and pass/fail thresholds.
```

## Disclaimer

All compliance content generated by this plugin is for informational and drafting purposes only. It does not constitute legal advice. The compliance officer is responsible for verifying all regulatory citations against current authoritative sources and exercising independent professional judgment before relying on this output for regulatory submissions or audit response.
