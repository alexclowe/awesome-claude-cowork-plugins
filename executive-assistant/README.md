# Executive Assistant Plugin

Triage inboxes, prep meetings, and draft follow-ups that keep executives moving.

Built by [The AI Career Lab](https://theaicareerlab.com/professions/executive-assistant) — AI tools, guides, and weekly digests designed for executive assistants and chiefs of staff.

## Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/inbox-triage` | Sort email into priorities, recommended replies, and escalation items |
| `/meeting-brief` | Create concise prep briefs with context, objectives, risks, and talking points |
| `/follow-up-draft` | Turn notes into recap emails with owners, deadlines, and next steps |
| `/daily-priorities` | Build a clear daily action plan from meetings, deadlines, and inbox pressure |

## Skills

This plugin includes skills that activate automatically when you're working on executive support tasks:

- **Executive Operations** — Calendar judgment, stakeholder coordination, travel logistics, priority triage, and meeting preparation
- **Communication & Coordination** — Inbox drafting, concise summaries, action-item tracking, tone matching, and polished follow-up communication

## Usage Examples

### Triage a Busy Inbox
```
/inbox-triage
Inbox context: CEO inbox with 41 unread messages.
Executive preferences: only surface items requiring a decision, draft replies for routine asks, and flag anything tied to board, customers, or hiring.
Messages include: board materials request, candidate scheduling, vendor contract follow-up, customer escalation, and internal planning notes.
```

### Build a Meeting Brief
```
/meeting-brief
Meeting: Q2 planning with COO, Head of Sales, Finance Director
When: Monday 9:00am
Context: pipeline is behind target, hiring plan may need to be cut 10%, board update due Thursday
Need: concise prep brief with stakeholder context, goals, likely friction points, and 5 questions the executive should ask
```

### Draft a Follow-Up Email
```
/follow-up-draft
Meeting notes: agreed to pause two vendor evaluations, move roadmap review to next Wednesday, and assign finance to model three headcount scenarios by Friday
Need: executive-style recap email with action items, owners, deadlines, and unresolved questions
```

### Build the Day Plan
```
/daily-priorities
Today's schedule: investor prep at 8:30, three hiring interviews, customer escalation at 2:00, dinner with partner prospects
Inbox pressure: 37 unread messages
Need: top 5 priorities, what to delegate, what can wait, and a suggested order of operations
```
